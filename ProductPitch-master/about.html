Ya Sure Ya Betcha!

We are the original "Uff Da Shoppe" in Stanwood, WA!

Established in 1981, this little Shoppe has delighted the local community, Scandinavians, and all nationalities for over 25 years.

Visitors enjoy shoppping while listening to music of many cultures, along with our own local celebrities, Stan Boreson and Harry Lindbeck.

We sell many items such as: Gnomes, trolls, vikings, lefse grills, krumkake irons, Scandinavian gifts, rosette sets, delft, lingonberry preserves, Pennsylvania Dutch hex signs, St. Nicks, Father Christmas, Scandinavian almond tins & Scandinavian cookbooks, Norwegian & Swedish language books, tomtens, nissens, julenissens, rommegrot pudding, rice pudding, fish balls, fiskeboller, kransekake rings, Suzanne tofhey tiles, flags, sandbakkelse tins, ginger & ballerina cookies, glogg, Camano Island gifts (shirts), and much more!
